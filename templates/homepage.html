Super Resolution

Image super resolution can be defined as increasing the size of small images while keeping the drop in quality to minimum, or restoring high resolution images from rich details obtained from low resolution images.

The key idea is to obtain the super-resolved image from observed low-resolution images. The super-resolution (SR) technique reconstructs a high-resolution image from single or multiple low-resolution images.
